Jonathan is currently a software engineer at Quorum, responsible for building out new features and improving the current application and infrastructure. Originally from Be'er Sheva, Israel, Jonathan grew up in New York and spent his college years at Cornell, studying Chemical Engineering and Computer Science. Jonathan is a member of Kappa Sigma and a three-time published author in peer-reviewed journals including Macromolecular Chemistry and Physics and the American Journal of Physiology, Renal Physiology. In his free time, Jonathan enjoys playing classical and jazz piano, mountain biking, motorcycles, and pretending to understand wine.
