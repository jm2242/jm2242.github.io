About Me

Hi, I'm Jonathan. I'm a student at Cornell University studying Chemical Engineering and Computer Science. In addition to English I am fluent in Hebrew and Russian.

I've always enjoyed solving problems and the process brings me enormous satisfaction and fullfillment. I hope to use my drive and determination to make a difference in the world.

I enjoy outdoor activities such as mountain biking, off road and street motorcycles, volleyball, and water skiing. I'm also an avid musician and have been playing piano since the age of 7.

I also give private tutoring sessions in Math, Chemistry, Physics, and Programming. See the tutoring link in the navigation bar for more details.
