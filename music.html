BACKGROUND


I began playing piano at the age of seven. I studied classical piano formally up until I left for college. In high school I expanded my musical interests greatly to jazz and rock/pop. I picked up the vibes, guitar, trombone, trumpet, and drums. I enjoy learning new instruments and hope to someday learn play saxophone.

My musical tastes are broad. Part of the time I listen to classical composers such as Tchaikovsky, Chopin, and Rachmaninov. I also listen to jazz. Some of my favorite artists are Sonny Rollins, Herbie Hancock, and Mel Torme. Another portion of the time I listen to classic rock bands such as Boston, Queen, and Pink Floyd.

At Cornell, I played baritone horn/trombone in the Jazz Ensemble my first semester. Presently, I am learning jazz and maintaining my classical piano chops.

Music is one of the most important elements of my life. In the future, I hope to play in a jazz group and as a keyboardist in a alternative/classic rock band.
